Midtown Cheap(ish) Eats

These are some of the many places I frequent for lunch and sometimes after work. I hope that you will enjoy them as much as I do.

    Go! Go! Curry
    Menu

    273 W 38th St, New York, NY 10018, USA

    Review: Japense Curry that sure is to knock your socks off. The chicken katsu, pork katsu, and fried shrimp all have a tasty, crunchy breading.

    Los Tacos No.1
    Menu

    229 W 43rd St, New York, NY 10036, USA

    Review: Best tacos in nyc. I'm not going back and forth and with anyone on this without proof. The pork is to die for and ask about the secret menu queso shell.

    City Kitchen
    Menu

    700 8th Ave, New York, NY 10036, USA

    Review: Hidden gem of vendors at the Row NYC. Cheese steaks, sushi, ramen, lobster, a taqueria, and dougnuts that will give you type 2 diabetes if you're not careful.

    Zoralie
    Menu

    449 W 51st St, New York, NY 10019, USA

    Review: The Domincan breakfast here is life changing (well if this is only Domincan food you've had). The lunch special is also steal at $9/$10 (The roast pork comes with amazing chicharron)

    Balade Your Way
    Menu

    144 W 37th St, New York, NY 10018, USA

    Review: Whether you pick ita, rice bowl, or salad you can't go wrong. The falafel, chicken shawarma, beef shawarma, and kebab all go perfectly with the bases. Don't forget the garlic whip ;).

    SpoonfedNYC
    Menu

    331 W 51st St, New York, NY 10019, USA

    Review: Midton Soulfood spot with many vegan, vegetarian, and gluten free friendly options. Great lunch specials and the perfect place to try jackfruit sliders for the first time.

    西安名吃 Xi'an Famous Foods
    Menu

    24 W 45th St, New York, NY 10036, USA

    Review: Food coming from the city of Xi’an, situated in the northwestern region of China. This NY chain has some of the best handmade noodles in New York.

    Otto's Tacos
    Menu

    705 9th Ave, New York, NY 10019, USA

    Review: What makes Otto's stand out amongst the many taco spots in nyc is definitly The Gorgan and it's tasty variety of meats.

    Minigrow by Honeygrow
    Menu

    285 Madison Ave, New York, NY 10017, USA

    Review: The bowls here make it easy to eat healthy in NYC

    El Rancho Burritos
    Menu

    1611, 359 W 45th St, New York, NY 10036, United States

    Review: El Rancho has a mom and pop like atmosphere and you can taste that feeling in the food

    Ponche Taqueria
    Menu

    420 W 49th St, New York, NY 10019, USA

    Review: Classic Mexican resturant

    Totto Ramen Hell's Kitchen
    Menu

    464 W 51st St, New York, NY 10019, USA

    Review: Ramen spot with beers and sake. Cash only at this location

    K-Chicken
    Menu

    528 9th Ave, New York, NY 10018, USA

    Review: Koren fried chicken. What else can I say? Try the lunch special!

    Black Burger
    Menu

    570 8th Ave, New York, NY 10018, USA

    Review: Burgers, curly fries, and shakes. The classic cheeseburger will not disappoint.

    Blue Maiz
    Menu

    606 8th Ave, New York, NY 10018, USA

    Review: I'm not sure which is my favorite, Tacos dorados or churro bites, but this place is amazing.

    Pio Pio 8
    Menu

    604 10th Ave, New York, NY 10036, USA

    Review: Come get your Peruvian fix, plus the lunch special is amazing and has a large portion.

    Kobeyaki
    Menu

    110 W 40th St, New York, NY 10018, USA

    Review: Japanese chain with bowls, sushi rolls, and burgers. Try the Kobeyaki burger made with wagyu beef!

    Crisp
    Menu

    110 W 40th St, New York, NY 10018, USA

    Review: Pita and waffle fries anyone?

    Katsu-Hama
    Menu

    11 E 47th St, New York, NY 10017, USA

    Review: An atomosphere and decor that makes me feel like I'm visiting Japan again. The katsu and curry here is to die for.

    Mr Bing
    Menu

    8 W 46th St, New York, NY 10036, USA

    Review: Bings, buns, and dumplings.... they will light up your taste buds. The maple bacon bing is interesting and a great alternative to the classic nyc b.e.c.

    Nino's 46
    Menu

    39 W 46th St, New York, NY 10036, USA

    Review: Nothing I write will do this place justice. The pasta is phenomenal. Please come try this classic Italian resturant.

    Café China
    Menu

    13 E 37th St, New York, NY 10016, USA

    Review: Traditional Chinese resturant with a michelin star and great lunch specials

    Red Moon
    Menu

    132 W 45th St, New York, NY 10036, USA

    Review: Hidden in a hotel lobby, Red Moon has great lunch specials and apparently good sushi.

    Divine Flavored Food Truck
    Menu

    828 2nd Ave, New York, NY 10017, USA

    Review: Amazing Nigerian food for lunch. I hope they open a sit down resturant soon

    The Bread Factory Cafe - Gio's Pizza
    Menu

    935 8th Ave, New York, NY 10019, USA

    Review: Is this technically in midtown? Anyway great pizza (that chicken bacon ranch!!!!!!).

    Turnstyle Underground Market
    Menu

    1000 8th Ave, New York, NY 10019, USA

    Review: Food hall in the subway? Many great options but Bolivian Llama Party has my belly. Their empandas melt in your mouth

    Urbanspace Vanderbilt
    Menu

    East 45th &, Vanderbilt Ave, New York, NY 10169, United States

    Review: Food hall in near Grand Central. Burgers, ramen, pizza, doughnuts, fried chicken, and many more amazing options. Shout out to Bobwhite Counter!

    Dig Inn
    Menu

    1407 Broadway, New York, NY 10018, USA

    Review: Honestly the bowls here are great, but you gotta try the mac n cheese, chicken thighs, and sweet potatoes.

    Fournos Theophilos
    Menu

    45 W 45th St, New York, NY 10036, USA

    Review: You can't go wrong here for lunch or just dessert. The chicken yeroo is amazing and you gotta try the mac n cheese.

    Jerk Pan
    Menu

    Park Ave & E 48th St, New York, NY 10017, United States

    Review: The best place for Jamaican food in midtown. The line outside this truck is proof enough, but the oxtails and curry goat will also convince you.
